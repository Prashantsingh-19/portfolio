# Bio — Who is Prashant Singh

Prashant Singh is an MBA candidate at BITS Pilani (Business Analytics) with a
B.Sc.(Hons) in Physics from Banaras Hindu University (BHU). His arc is:
Physics → Data → Strategy. He designs data architectures and analytics products
that turn raw information into strategic decisions.

He describes himself as a product thinker who builds at the intersection of
data and decisions — comfortable defining system schemas, directing builds,
and drawing business implications from complex data.
