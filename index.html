Prashant Singh

My foundation is physics at BHU — rigorous training in first-principles reasoning, mathematical modeling of complex systems, and the discipline to distinguish signal from noise. That mindset is the bedrock of everything I build.

Today, as an MBA candidate at BITS Pilani specializing in Business Analytics, I operate at the confluence of quantitative rigor and strategic clarity. I design data architecture, build analytics dashboards that drive decisions, and translate technical findings into business implications — connecting the pipeline to the boardroom.

My long-term focus is corporate finance consulting — the discipline where technical depth meets strategic leverage. I aim to be the person who not only designs the system but also determines what the numbers mean for the business. Never mistaking noise for implication.
